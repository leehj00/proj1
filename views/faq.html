Frequently Asked Questions


Do you offer custom cakes?

Yes! We specialize in custom cakes for all occasions. Contact us for more details.

How far in advance should I place an order?

We recommend placing your order at least 3 days in advance for standard cakes and 1 week for custom designs.

Do you have vegan or gluten-free options?

Yes, we offer both vegan and gluten-free cakes. Please inform us of any dietary restrictions.

Do you deliver?

Yes, we offer delivery within a 10-mile radius. Additional charges may apply.
